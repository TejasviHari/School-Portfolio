What you need

my class board

it is an app since now its online

My class board is an application for our assignments, summaries, tests,etc. We call my class board as MCB app for short. The thing I like most of it is that we can send pictures of our work and the teacher can correct the image by drawing over it through the computer. You might not understand that but trust me it is sooooo cool!! If you are thinking of joining Global edge then you better have this because it's all online.

it is an app since now its online

Codetantra is where I take my online classes it has breakout rooms, polls, webcams,microphones,multi-user drawing boards, Public and private chat,etc.You can also set your status like happy,sad,confused,away,raise hand. The Global Edge School

The Global Edge School, Narsingi under the aegis of the Sri M B R Educational Society stands in its sprawling campus with green floral plants and huge playgrounds enhancing the picturesque setting. This is the fourth school under the banner of the Sri MBR Educational Society. The building, which houses modern classrooms, activity rooms and learning center provides a welcoming and stimulating environment for learning.
